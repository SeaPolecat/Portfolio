machinE

Sept - Nov 2023

In a world overrun by machinery, a mysterious entity summons you, the last human, to collect sacred engine parts for a spooky ritual… Created with Gamemaker, machinE offers a puzzle platformer experience with environmental storytelling.

This project won first place and a $350 cash prize at a Boston University game jam. As a programmer, I worked alongside passionate artists and learned a lot about the Gamemaker engine. machinE is currently in development, but we plan on completing it in the near future.

Treehopping with Benjy

Jun - Dec 2023

Play Now ▶

Oh no, Benjy the Treehopper's forest is about to be destroyed! Hop around branches to find the last tree seeds so he can replant a new home.

This game was for a summer internship at Idori, a startup company focused on sustainability education. Our goal was to help children love nature through a Club Penguin style virtual world, and Treehopping with Benjy is one of the minigames to be included in that world.

Gimme Cards

Mar 2021 - Present

Visit Website ▶

Gimme Cards is a Discord bot that lets users collect and trade virtual Pokémon cards on the go, free of charge. Made with the Java Discord API (JDA), its goal is to promote social interaction among Pokémon TCG enthusiasts.

I started this project during the pandemic, because people did not visit in-person stores to buy Pokémon cards as often. Gimme Cards eventually became verified by the Discord Developer Program, gaining 150+ players in over 200 servers.
