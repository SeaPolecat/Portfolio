Welcome to my Gallery!

My name is Wayne Wang, and I'm a third-year student at Boston University majoring in Business Administration and Computer Science.

In the future, I want to become a video game developer, because I believe that video games have the power to influence generations and bring people together as friends. I've worked on 2D indie games using Gamemaker and Unity, and plan on making a 3D game in Unreal Engine soon. At BU's Board Games Club, I help design escape room puzzles and write scripts for mini plays. Other times, I enjoy experimenting with pixel art and Blender.

In my free time, I explore Boston with my friends or play indie games on Steam. Some of my favourite games include Sky: Children of the Light, Palworld, and Hell Let Loose.
